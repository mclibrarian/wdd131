Timeline of American Girl

1986

Pleasant Company is founded; Kirsten, Samantha, and Molly are released.

1989

American Girl headquarters moves to Middleton, Wisconsin.

1991

The five-year anniversary; Felicity is released.

1993

Addy is released.

1995

American Girl of Today is released. This line will eventually be renamed Just Like You, My American Girl, and finally Truly Me. It is comprised of non-character dolls that are often called "lookalikes", although they can't be customized.

1996

The ten-year anniversary; American Girl's website is launched.

1997

Josefina is released.

1998

Pleasant Company is sold to Mattel; American Girl Place Chicago opens.

2000

Kit is released.

2001

The fifteen-year anniversary; Lindsey, the first Girl of the Year, is released. Girls of the Year are contemporary characters with stories set in the year they are released.

2002

Kaya is released; Girls of Many Lands is launched. This was a line of 9-inch dolls with stories set in other countries between 1592 and 1939.

2004

Pleasant Company is renamed American Girl; Samantha: An American Girl Holiday is released; Nellie, the first Best Friend doll, is released.

2005

Felicity: An American Girl Adventure is released; Elizabeth is released; Girls of Many Lands is discontinued.

2006

The twenty-year anniversary; Molly: An American Girl on the Home Front is released; Emily is released.

2007

Julie and Ivy are released.

2008

Kit Kittredge: An American Girl is released; Ruthie is released.

2009

Rebecca is released.

2011

The twenty-fifth-year anniversary; Marie-Grace and Cécile are released.

2012

Caroline is released.

2014

BeForever is launched. This was a rebrand of the Historical line and involved retiring some characters and combining the six-book series into two books. Later characters would be released with two books instead of six.

2015

Maryellen is released.

2016

The thirty-year anniversary; Melody is released.

2017

Nanea is released; the Create-Your-Own line, which allows people to make custom dolls, is released; the Contempory Characters line is released. This was a very short-lived line that had three characters with stories set in 2017, when the line launched.

2019

BeForever turns back into the Historical Characters line.

2020

Courtney is released.

2021

The thirty-fifth-year anniversary; American Girl is inducted into the National Toy Hall of Fame; World By Us is launched. This was another line of contemporary characters, containing three characters who are best friends and whose books build on each other, unlike previous contemporary lines.

2022

Claudie is released.

2023

Isabel and Nicki are released.

2024

American Girl History is launched; Dolls based on the Disney princesses are released.
